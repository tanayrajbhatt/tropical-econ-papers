Understanding Preferences: "Demand Types", and the Existence of Equilibrium with Indivisibilities

Elizabeth Baldwin, Paul Klemperer

Econometrica · 2019

Abstract

We provide a new taxonomy of valuations over indivisible goods using tropical geometry and the theory of tropical hypersurface arrangements, characterizing which classes of preferences guarantee competitive equilibrium existence.

Product-Mix Auctions and Tropical Geometry

Ngoc Mai Tran, Josephine Yu

Mathematics of Operations Research · 2019

Abstract

We use tropical geometry to analyze the product-mix auction of Klemperer, giving a geometric characterization of competitive equilibrium and its existence conditions via tropical hypersurfaces.
